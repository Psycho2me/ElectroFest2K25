About ElectroFest2K25

Welcome to ElectroFest2K25, the annual National-Level Technical Symposium hosted by the Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE). With a commitment to innovation and excellence, this event brings together the brightest minds in the field of technology, innovation, and creativity.

Where Innovation Meets Excitement

Our event offers a unique blend of technical and non-technical challenges, providing a platform for students, professionals, and enthusiasts to showcase their skills, compete in dynamic events, and collaborate with like-minded individuals. From cutting-edge technology to mind-boggling quizzes and interactive workshops, ElectroFest2K25 is the place where ideas come to life!

What to Expect:

  • A wide range of events: From hands-on workshops, robotics competitions, paper presentations, to creative contests like photography and ad presentations, there is something for everyone.
  • Prizes and recognition: Participate and stand a chance to win exciting prizes, certificates, and accolades that will take your profile to the next level.
  • Networking opportunities: Meet industry experts, academicians, and fellow students. Build connections that could shape your future.

Join us to explore the world of technology, push boundaries, and create lasting memories! Whether you’re looking to test your skills or simply experience the thrill of innovation, ElectroFest2K25 has it all.

About ElectroFest2K25

ElectroFest2K25 is a celebration of innovation, creativity, and technology. The Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering presents this exciting symposium with a range of tech and non-tech events designed to challenge and entertain you!

Join us for a series of events that will test your technical skills, creative thinking, and teamwork. From project expos to quirky tongue twisters, ElectroFest2K25 promises to be an event to remember!

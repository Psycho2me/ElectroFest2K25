About ElectroFest 2K25

ElectroFest 2K25 is the signature technical symposium organized by the Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering. This event is a fusion of creativity, innovation, and engineering excellence, designed to ignite young minds and foster collaboration. Featuring a variety of technical and non-technical events, workshops, and challenges, ElectroFest 2K25 offers students an exciting platform to showcase their skills, exchange knowledge, and build professional networks. Whether you're passionate about circuits, coding, robotics, or just ready to test your problem-solving skills, ElectroFest 2K25 promises an electrifying experience for all tech enthusiasts!

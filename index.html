General Instructions for All Events

  • Register through the provided registration links.
  • Arrive 30 minutes before your event starts.
  • Bring your ID and any required materials.
  • Participants may register for up to 2 events.
  • For queries, contact event coordinators listed below.
  • Event-specific rules are mentioned in each event card.
  • Food and water will be provided at the venue.
  • Follow the event schedule strictly.
  • Respect the venue and maintain cleanliness.
  • In case of any issues, contact the event coordinators.
  • All participants must adhere to the code of conduct.
  • Winners will be announced at the end of each event.
  • Certificates will be provided to all participants.
  • Prizes will be awarded to the top performers.
  • Follow us on social media for updates and announcements.

Instruction

● ID card is necessary for all the participants.

● Participants can register as individuals or as team (Max 4 per team).

● Participants can register for multiple events.

● On-spot registration available.

● Registration fee per head is 100.

● Participation certificates will be given as E-certificate.

● Usage of mobile/internet in events without any prior permission is strictly prohibited.

● The judge decision is final and cannot be contested.

● Top three prizes will be awarded with hardcopy certificates and medals
